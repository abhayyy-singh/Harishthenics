Who is HARISTHENICS?

HARISTHENICS is a movement-based system created by Harish Sharma. Haris represents the person behind the work, and thenics represents movement. Together, HARISTHENICS stands for movements thoughtfully designed for real human bodies. It is not about copying exercises. It is about curating movements that suit you. What HARISTHENICS Works On HARISTHENICS movements are designed to support every aspect of human movement, including: Strength & Control Mobility & Flexibility Rehabilitation & Recovery Muscle Release & Tissue Health Posture Correction Muscle Imbalance Correction Skill Development Pain Management & Injury Prevention Every movement is designed with purpose, awareness, and progression The HARISTHENICS Ideology At HARISTHENICS, there is no "right" or "wrong" movement. There is only: The movement that suits your body The movement that does not‚Äîyet Movement should be designed based on: Your body's current strength & flexibility Your pain, limitations, or past injuries Your goals and daily lifestyle demands We focus on Which movement is needed and which is not Why a movement feels restricted or uncomfortable How to use progression and regression intelligently When to change movement patterns to avoid future muscle imbalances This approach ensures long-term results, not temporary fixes. My Mission My biggest aim is simple To help you move better, live better, and stay pain-free for life. Through HARISTHENICS, I help people: Understand their bodies Build confidence in movement Reduce pain without fear Move with strength, freedom, and awareness Movement is not just exercise. Movement is quality of life.
